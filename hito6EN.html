Plaça del Mirador


The Plaza del Mirador is the nerve centre of the S'Agaró urban development and provides a spectacular view of the Mediterranean, in the heart of the camí de ronda.



The square retains the architectural style of Masó and Ensesa, and also the atmosphere and warmth of the urban development’s years of splendour. The tranquility of the landscape and its surroundings make this one of the favourite places for visitors, who come here for an enjoyable rest, just as they did in their day such Hollywood stars as Ava Gardner, Frank Sinatra, Anthony Quinn and John Wayne when they were shooting their films or spending the summer at the Hostel de la Gavina.
